Definition 3.1.2. Vector Field in \(3\)-Dimensions.

Vector Field in \(3\)-Dimensions Let \(\vcF\) be a function from \(\bbr^3\to\bbr^3\) such that
\begin{equation*} \vcF(x,y,z)=\bmat{P(x,y,z)\\Q(x,y,z)\\R(x,y,z)}=P(x,y,z)\vci+Q(x,y,z)\vcj+R(x,y,z)\vck. \end{equation*}
We call \(\vcF\) a vector field from \(\bbr^3\) to \(\bbr^3\text{.}\)
